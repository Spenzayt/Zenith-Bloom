# Zenith Bloom

## Overview

<img width="1920" height="1080" alt="image" src="https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/05366990-9d68-4e29-9903-59cd690249e1" />

**Zenith Bloom** is a 2D action-platformer developed in C++ with **SFML**, set in a solarpunk world where technology and nature coexist.  
You play as a high-tech cyborg tasked with stopping rogue machines that pollute a solar-powered city and repairing the damage they leave behind.

## Features

- **Solarpunk Universe**: A bright, green city powered entirely by solar energy, mixing nature and advanced technology.
- **Action-Platformer Gameplay**:
  - Fast-paced movement with jumps, double jumps, and boosts.
  - Platforming inspired by classic 2D games.
- **Energy Management System**:
  - Every action consumes energy.
  - Energy regenerates in sunlit areas but causes overheating.
  - Shade zones allow the cyborg to cool down.
- **Combat System**:
  - Melee attacks.
  - Energy projectiles (straight shots, charged shots).
- **Repair Mechanics**:
  - Restore damaged structures.
  - Replant vegetation using special abilities.
- **Day/Night Cycle**:
  - Day: Machines are active and dangerous.
  - Night: Machines shut down, allowing safer exploration and repairs.

## Gameplay

1. **Explore the City**: Move from left to right through a fully vegetated futuristic city.
2. **Fight Rogue Machines**: Defeat polluting machines using melee and ranged attacks.
3. **Manage Energy & Heat**: Balance sunlight for energy regeneration while avoiding overheating.
4. **Repair the Environment**:
   - Fix damaged structures.
   - Replant vegetation to clean polluted zones.
5. **Complete the Level**: A level is finished only when all damage has been repaired and nature has been restored.

---

## Enemies

- **Extractor**: Stationary machine releasing toxic fumes.
- **Crusher**: Mobile machine that destroys the environment.
- **Incinerator**: Spreads fire and burning zones.
- **Spreader Drone**: Flying enemy dropping toxic or flammable substances.

Enemies are active in sunlight and stop functioning at night, encouraging strategic gameplay.

## Victory Condition

- Repair all damaged structures.
- Restore all polluted zones with vegetation.
- Reach 100% restoration to complete the level.
